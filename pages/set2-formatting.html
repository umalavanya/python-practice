Question 1:

What is the output of the following code snippet?

age = 25 name = "Alice" print(f"My name is {name} and I am {age} years old.")

Question 2:

What will be the output of the following code?

name = "John" age = 30 print(f"Hello, {name}. You are {age + 5} years old.")

Question 3:

What is the output of the following code?

name = "Emily" age = 28 print("Hello, {}. You are {} years old.".format(name, age))

Question 4:

What will be the output of the following code?

name = "Sophia" age = 22 print("My name is {}. I am {} years old.".format(name, age))

Question 5:

What will be the output of the following code?

name = "Alice" age = 30 print("{:<10} is {:>5} years old.".format(name, age))

num = 10.5678 print("{:.2f}".format(num))
